# Rebalance Key Crafting

Chest keys in Core Keeper are single-use — one key per locked chest — so
crafting them again and again quietly burns through your bars. This mod
rebalances that cost down to something far more reasonable.

With **Rebalance Key Crafting**, every tier chest key costs just **a quarter**
of its vanilla materials to craft (never less than 1 of each ingredient). The
keys themselves are untouched: same keys, same chests, same loot — they are
simply much cheaper to make, so opening that next vault doesn't mean another
trip to the smelter.

## What it does

- Crafting any tier chest key — **Copper, Iron, Scarlet, Octarine, Galaxite, Solarite, Relucite** — now costs ~25% of the vanilla materials (minimum 1 of each ingredient).
- The workbench shows the reduced cost, and that is exactly what gets consumed — no mismatch between what you see and what you pay.
- Purely a cost tweak: keys, chests, drop rates and everything else behave exactly like vanilla.
- No dependencies, no extra setup — just install and craft.

## Multiplayer

Install the mod on **both the client and the server**.
